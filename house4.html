Property Description

The apartment is located below the Winterberg Clinic on Theodor-Heuss-Strasse, on the 1st floor of a well-maintained multi-family building with 46 units.

The apartment also includes a basement storage room and a garage.
Both the bathroom and guest WC are internal (without windows).
