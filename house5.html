House 3

Property Description

This charming maisonette apartment is located in a well-maintained three-family house.
On the ground floor of this apartment, there is a guest WC, easily accessible for visitors.
The spacious living room provides plenty of space for relaxing and can be arranged in various ways.
Directly next to the living room is the dining room, which serves as a central connection between the living room and kitchen.
From the dining room, a door leads directly to the terrace, which is perfect for outdoor dining or relaxation.
The terrace flows seamlessly into the garden, providing additional space for leisure activities.
On the first floor, there are three bedrooms.
One of the bedrooms is designed as a connecting room, meaning you pass through it to reach another room.
This layout offers flexible usage options, such as a study or dressing room.
The other bedroom is separate and ideal as a children's room, providing ample space for play and privacy.
Additionally, there is a spacious, modern bathroom with natural light, creating a pleasant atmosphere through its windows.
An additional room, not counted as part of the living area, is accessible from the first floor. This room can be used in various ways, such as storage for various items or as a hobby room for creative projects. This extra space provides practical opportunities for individualized use.
The apartment also includes a garage, offering extra space for a vehicle or storage.
It is located directly next to the building and is easily accessible.
